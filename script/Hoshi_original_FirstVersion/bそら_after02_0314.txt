We're looking up at the heavens, feeling the cold winter air on our skin.
％Ｂ
「Ohh! The stars look great!」
Winter's still hanging around for March.
Sora and I have come to the campground we all went to back in high school.
％Ｂ
「Winter stargazing's great. I feel like I can see the stars way clearer than in summer.」

Sora
「...Winter air is clearer. It's dryer and has fewer air currents, and is more stable. There's hardly any dust or water vapor getting in the way in the air.」


％Ｂ
「So it's not just me, then.」
Sora
「...*nod*」
Sora
「...But why did you suddenly want to come here?」
％Ｂ
「I've been real busy with all the commission stuff for work lately, right? So I haven't gotten to spend any time with you.」

Sora
「I've been busy too... I haven't been able to cook for you because the new planetarium show's almost ready to air.」


％Ｂ
「You don't need to worry about that. But I don't think I'll be able to take a longer break from work for a while.」

％Ｂ
「I think things are only gonna get busier with Shuuichi's commission. I know it's just some place close to where we live, but I wanted to see the stars together with you while I still can.」


Sora
「...Being busy is a good thing. I won't complain if you can't go out to do things.」

Sora
「It's enough just getting to see your face, even for a little. Holding hands is enough to make me happy.」

Sora
「It's enough just getting to see your face, even for a little. Holding hands is enough to make my happy.」

She takes my hand.
My hand, which had been chilled in the winter air, slowly grows warmer.
％Ｂ
「For now, the most I can do is see the stars with you here, where you can see them the best in Shionagi. But let's go on a trip again once things slow down at work.」

Sora
「...*nod*」
％Ｂ
「Oh, and... Here you go.」
I hand her a small wrapped gift.
Sora
「...What's this...?」
％Ｂ
「Today's White Day. This is in return for Valentine's.」
Sora makes me star-shaped cookies every Valentine's Day.

Those same ones named after stars that she gave me back when we were stargazing in high school. They only grow more impressive every year.

I gotta say, I was beyond amazed that time she managed to replicate Jupiter's stripes...
％Ｂ
「I know I always give you candy or marshmallows, but I went for something a little different this time.」

Sora
「...Can I open it?」
％Ｂ
「Of course.」
She opens the box.
Sora
「...!」
She looks at me with wide eyes.
Sora
「It's... stars...!」
They're star-shaped earrings.
％Ｂ
「I got paid for that first remodeling job. It's not like it's my first paycheck ever or anything, but I wanted to buy you something with the money I got for the first time after becoming independent.」


Sora
「...Ryou...」
Sora
「...Really...」
％Ｂ
「I picked those 'cause stars always suit you the best. I think the dangling design makes them look like they're floating like real stars.」

Sora
「I'm so happy... Can I put them on?」
％Ｂ
「Yeah, show me how they look.」
Sora happily brings the earrings up to eye level, looks at them for a while, and then puts them on her ears.

Sora
「...How do I look?」
％Ｂ
「You look great.」
I give her a thumbs-up.
Sora
「...This makes me really happy.」
％Ｂ
「I'm glad.」
Stars glimmer above us in the heavens.
And stars swing at Sora's sides.
But─
Sora
「Ryou... Thank you.」
Sora
「...Thank you.」
Sora smiles a smile that outshines all of those stars, and softly closes her eyes.

all
I gently kiss her.
To place yet another small memory over all the others we've stacked up already, beneath the winter night sky.
